My services

I offer a lighting design and a lighting operation service. Below is more information about them.

Lighting design

The right lighting design is important for your show, which is why I aim to incorporate your visions, while having the technical know-how for how to put it all together.

I will keep you informed about the progress of the design, and regularly contact you for any changes that you wish to be made.

After the design is completed, I will send all of the relevant paperwork to you, such as lighting plots and show files.

This service includes:

Regular communication

Incorporation of your visions

Lighting plots

Renders of design

Show Files

Lighting operation
