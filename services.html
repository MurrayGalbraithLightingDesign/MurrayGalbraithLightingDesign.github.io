Lighting Designer

Whether you are a band, choir or a theatre performance, the right lighting is important for your show. My lighting designs incorporate your visions, while being technically detailed and ready for construction. This service includes:


🖋️ Lighting plot

🖼️ Renders of your design

🗣️ Communication with you about progress of the design and changes that could be made

Lighting Operator
