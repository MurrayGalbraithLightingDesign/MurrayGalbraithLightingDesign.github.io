Lighting operation

For this show me and a team put together sound and lighting for the Helping Hands Group's production of 'Once Upon A Pantomime'. There was only a 2 hour get in, and it was a pleasure to work with such organised and professional teams, both in-house and Young Technicians Academy.

Before the show included changing a number of lighting ques which where different to previs, and replacing a faulty DMX cable for the upstage led batterns.

During the show I operated the lighting on an Avolites Tiger Touch II, with lighting cues called out by the DSM via QLab.

The audience really enjoyed the show, and because of it's success they are going to do it again next year.
